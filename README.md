# Granny Boom

**The game that lets you slay aliens while you improve your writing.**

A guided writing tool for 8–12 year-old native English speakers who need help moving past basic sentences into sharper, more vivid prose — with a 60-second arcade chaser starring Granny and her cupcake nukes.

## How it works

- Pick a topic (**Sport, Adventure, Mystery, Sci-Fi**) and a story.
- Each story is a **5-sentence narrative paragraph**.
- For each sentence, you're shown a fixed opener and a few containers of word/phrase choices.
- Pick the **best option** in each container — distractors are designed to teach against vague nouns, basic verbs, filter words and tell-not-show writing.
- Press **Check**. Wrong picks turn red so you can fix them.
- Once the sentence is correct, **type it out** word-for-word as reinforcement.
- After all 5 sentences, your **full paragraph** is revealed and saved to **My Stories**.
- Finishing a story unlocks a **60-second Forge Invaders arcade reward**.

## Forge Invaders

A retro space-shooter you earn the right to play. Features:

- Four player ships unlocked with XP (Mk I → Vanguard Mk IV).
- **Granny Boom** unlocks at a **7-day login streak** — she lobs nuke cupcakes with curving trajectories, smoke trails and a wide AOE blast.
- Five distinct monster ranks, a swooping mother ship, and the **Grand-Daddy** boss who arrives after five cleared waves.
- Beat the boss to enter **HYPER MODE** — faster, twitchier, hotter.

## Stories

24 stories total — 6 per topic, split across 3 difficulty levels (L1, L2, L3).

## Run locally

Just open `index.html` in a browser. All state is saved in `localStorage`.
